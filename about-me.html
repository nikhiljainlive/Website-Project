About Me!

Nikhil Jain is an aspiring artist and technical student who belongs to Bhopal city of state Madhya Pradesh, in the heart of India. Nikhil is an inspiring poet and growing artist who loves to turn his imagination into wonderful creations.

"Poetry was the most significant turn in my life, I'm a poet who grew to adopt a love of literature." he says.
.

Nikhil's favourite hobby is to meet artists from different places and cultures and make friends. His poems are being published on many websites online.
