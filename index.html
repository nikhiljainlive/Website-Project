Self Introduction

I am Nikhil Jain and I belong to Bhopal city of Madhya Pradesh. I have completed my Bachelors of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering Stream from Jai Narain College of Technology, Bhopal.

If I talk about my Strengths, I am motivated, self driven and determined to learn and grow in a growing organization. I am adaptive to learn different technologies.

My Hobbies include writing Poetry, reading Books, Singing, listening to music and Adventure Tourism. I wish to explore the World some day.
